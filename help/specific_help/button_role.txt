**Explanation:**
Creates a button that assigns a role to the user.
It will create a new message from the bot that has the button.

**Permissions needed:**
Bot: `manage_roles`
User: `administrator`

**Usage example:**
`/button_role [message_text] [label] [role]`

**Options:**
`message_text` - The text the message should have.
`label` - The text the button should have.
`role` - The role to give when the button is clicked.

`[]` - required
`()` - optional
